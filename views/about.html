About us

Christopher Borges

In a recent job interview I was asked to describe myself in five words. I said “scientist, visionary, writer, Taurus, and musician” in that order. I’m Christopher Borges, I’m a Film/Philosophy/English triple major taking a few gap years while I find myself and explore the supernatural.

When I’m not filming, I’m reading, writing, lifting, or playing with my The Dresden Dolls cover band called “The Maybe Virginias.” I love cats, coffee, the Legend of Zelda, and open mindedness.

Connor Lloyd Falkner

Connor is a Political Science major, currently best known for his work saying “actually” a lot at parties. Prior to joining an occult investigation team, Connor considered a career in comedy before realizing that no one else would ever find him as funny as he finds himself. When Connor isn’t busy expanding the frontiers of modern day skepticism, he enjoys writing descriptions of himself in the third person, going to the gym (CAN YOU TELL?), and managing the University of Central Florida’s 14th best non-competitive bar trivia team.
